    Rules of the game:
  • The objective is to transfer all disks to the right hand tower in the fewest possible moves.
  • Only one disk at the top of a tower can be moved at a time to the top of another tower.
  • A larger disk can not be placed on top of a smaller disk.
  • Three towers are used to solve this puzzle:
    • Source - where disks begin
    • Auxiliary - temporary tower
    • Destination - where disks end
